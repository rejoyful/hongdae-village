Asset type: first playable character for an isometric pixel-art web game

Composition/framing: exactly one character centered, full body visible, generous

Lighting/mood: soft warm rim light from upper-left, restrained shadows inside the

Color palette: charcoal, warm bone, concrete gray, muted sage, a single muted

Scene/backdrop: perfectly flat solid `#00ff00` chroma-key background for

Constraints: no cast shadow, no contact shadow, no pedestal, no environment, no
props except the folded plan and pouch, no additional views, no sprite sheet, no
UI, no border, no logo, no watermark, no text; do not use `#00ff00` anywhere in

The chroma-key background was removed locally, then the transparent bounds were
